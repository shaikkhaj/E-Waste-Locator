    List of E-Waste Devices and Their Harmful Elements

    SmartPhones

    Smartphones contain several toxic substances that pose risks to both human health and the environment.

    Laptop

    Laptops include a range of hazardous materials.Laptops share similar toxic components.

    Refrigerator

    Refrigerators, especially older models, contain substances like chlorofluorocarbons (CFCs) or hydrochlorofluorocarbons (HCFCs) used as refrigerants.

    Television

    Televisions, particularly older CRT models, are among the most toxic electronics. Lead is heavily used in the glass tubes and solder, posing serious risks to neurological health.

    Other

    The improper disposal of these devices, such as dumping in landfills or burning in informal recycling sites, leads to the release of these substances into the environment.
