    E-Waste Impact

    Common harmful components in e-waste:

    • Lead - Nervous system damage
    • Mercury - Brain and kidney damage
    • Cadmium - Lung damage and cancer risk
    • PCBs - Liver damage and cancer

    Proper recycling prevents these toxins from entering our environment.
